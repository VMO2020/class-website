WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Every design seeks to either create a sense of unity among different elements, or creatively break that unity to encourage a particular action. The principles covered here will help you do both. Thankfully, there are a few simple but powerful guidelines for creating — and disrupting — unified designs. Our Book: Aims to introduce this and establish clearly the rules.
